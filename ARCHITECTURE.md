# Architecture — v2.6.0 Slow Energy

## Friend's Soul

Friend's Soul is composed of:

- Relationship: shared time and history. It never decreases.
- Mood: current feeling. It changes naturally and frequently.
- Energy: emotional room for effort. It changes slowly.
- Life Rhythm: local time rhythm. Morning, day, evening, night.
- Season: local seasonal feeling.

## Energy Model

Energy is not HP, stamina, or a game resource. It is the Friend's emotional room to face the user.

### Energy decreases slowly

Tasks use a small amount of Energy:

- Light questions: very small cost.
- Research, summaries, study, translation, planning: small cost.
- Heavy creation, long writing, coding, images: medium cost.

### Energy recovers slowly

- Touching the Friend barely restores Energy. It mainly changes Mood.
- Casual conversation restores a little.
- Warm words such as thanks restore a little more.
- Time, sleep, and mornings slowly restore Energy.

### Relationship softens effort

The deeper the Relationship, the less Energy tasks require.
This represents the Friend becoming more comfortable being with the user, not becoming stronger.

## Appearance Formula

Appearance = Mood × Energy × Relationship × Life Rhythm × Season

Appearance is not manual dress-up. It is the Friend's current inner state appearing outside.
